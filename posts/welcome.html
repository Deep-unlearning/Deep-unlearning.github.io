Why "Deep Unlearning"?

The concept of unlearning in machine learning fascinates me. Just as neural networks learn patterns from data, understanding how they can selectively "forget" or unlearn specific information is crucial for privacy, security, and model maintenance.

Beyond the technical meaning, "unlearning" also represents being open to questioning assumptions and updating our understanding when presented with new information.
